## F2 — ontology-driven expression

Replace the gp.rs weight flat-index match at lines 97-116 with a per-species ordered TraitMap. New types in gp.rs: LocusSelector enum with ByIndex of LocusId or ByGoAnchor of GoTermId; TraitBinding holding a trait, a locus selector, and a param id; TraitMap holding an ordered Vec of TraitBinding, never a HashMap; OntologyMap holding a TraitMap and implementing GenotypePhenotypeMap. Resolution is pure, ordered, no RNG: per binding resolve the locus (ByIndex via genome.locus id linear scan at lib.rs:189; ByGoAnchor via the first locus in Vec order whose go_refs contains the anchor), find the param by id, call as_unit_scalar, clamp to 0..1; a missing locus or param yields a documented 0.0 and never a panic. A trait reads the locus and param its species TraitMap names, not a global flat slot. Selection is unchanged: reset_with_genome at lib.rs:411-412 and with_genome_and_rng at lib.rs:667-668 still call express then get GrowthRate. Default identity: default_plant_trait_map is the 9 ByIndex bindings GrowthRate to L0 P0, Stature to L0 P1, Branchiness to L0 P2, LeafSize to L1 P0, LeafHue to L1 P1, Reflectance to L1 P2, Fecundity to L2 P0, DroughtTolerance to L3 P0, KillSwitchLinkage to L3 P1; each resolved scalar equals today flat scalar. Verified: both sim-core call sites use the concrete type WeightedSumMap express, so keep WeightedSumMap as a thin wrapper delegating to OntologyMap with default_plant_trait_map, leaving the call sites and the GenotypePhenotypeMap seam untouched. The plant stays ByIndex because ByGoAnchor cannot disambiguate the three params of L0 under one go_ref without adding per-param tags, which would be a re-pin; ByGoAnchor is reserved for new species. The Trait enum appends microbe variants only and never reorders the 9, since Trait ALL order is the Phenotype and render order. express iterates the species bindings; the plant binding order stays identical to Trait ALL. Placement: ship in-core const TraitMaps now with no schema churn so default.json stays byte-identical; design but do not yet add an optional serde-default trait_map block on SpeciesSpec. Golden test in the F2 commit: WeightedSumMap express of sample_genome equals OntologyMap default express and GrowthRate is 0.6; the determinism pin and the gp.rs phenotype tests stay green unmodified.

## Microbe trait set

The E. coli TraitMap over its 136 loci, selection-driver first. One, GrowthRate, the universal selection trait and the only one selection reads. It binds a growth-backbone locus and param; WT activity is derived from the FBA biomass objective R_Biomass_Ecoli_core with mu_max about 0.87 to 0.97 per hour on glucose-minimal aerobic, not invented, normalized to 0..1. Drivers are glycolysis plus TCA plus OxPhos: pfkA or pfkB, gapA, eno, pykF or pykA, gltA, icd, sucAB, atp operon. Two, GlucoseUptake, decorative, from the PTS cluster ptsG b1101, crr b2417, ptsHI b2415-2416, malX b1621, manXYZ b1817-1819 to GLCpts; the EX_glc__D_e cap is Vmax. Three, RespirationMode, an Enum or Bool aerobic versus anaerobic, from aerobic genes cbdAB b0978 and b0979, cydAB b0733 and b0734, NADH16 nuoA-N, versus fermentation genes PFL pflB b0903 and frd b4151-4154; O2 gates the switch at runtime and the regulation is Ring-2. Four, AcetateOverflow, the Layer-3 mineralization tap, from pta b2297 and b2458 plus ackA b2296 and b1849 to EX_ac_e; threshold-linear in mu above about 0.76 per hour. Five, FermentationCapacity, decorative, from ldhA b1380 plus adhE b1241. Each decorative trait reads distinct loci so one edit moves one trait. Microbe variants are appended to the Trait enum after the 9 plant traits. Out of scope for the metabolic core because they are not FBA-driven: AcidStressTolerance rpoS and Motility or chemotaxis fliC, motA, cheA; both are real but absent from the 136 core genes, and if wanted later they become extra so_term 704 loci with distinct GO tags and author-set values, a separate decision.


## Re-pin analysis

HASH-NEUTRAL, zero re-pin, not a stop-the-line on the recommended path. Exactly two things feed the pinned hash. First, BaseGrowthRate equals WeightedSumMap express of genome then get GrowthRate, unwrap_or 0.5, at lib.rs:411-412, re-expressed identically at lib.rs:667-668, driving selection and allele_freq. Second, genome parameter_count folded into hash_world as genome_params, read at lib.rs:778. The other 8 plant traits are render-only. The identity TraitMap resolves GrowthRate to L0 P0, the same Numeric 0.6 with min 0 max 1, giving 0.6 byte-identical, and sample_genome is untouched so parameter_count stays 9; the default run is byte-identical and the pinned literal holds. The tests growth_rate_tracks_first_parameter at gp.rs:176-181, all_trait_values_in_unit_range, get_returns_each_trait, and the lib.rs pin all pass verbatim. Contrast, the path not taken: ByGoAnchor for the plant needs per-param GO anchors on sample_genome, which mutates go_refs and bytes and the round-tripped default.json and could shift GrowthRate; that is a deliberate re-pin with a new literal plus a history ledger line plus a DECISIONS ADR plus a stop-the-line per inv 3. The ADR-017 draft reportedly pre-marks F2 as a re-pin and stop-the-line; this code-verified analysis shows it need not be, so the human should confirm the hash-neutral path and amend the ADR. ecoli.json is independently hash-neutral because it is not the default species and does not feed the hash.

## Invariant risks
- inv 3: the hash-neutral verdict rests on express of sample_genome staying byte-identical and parameter_count staying 9; any sample_genome byte or tag change, or plant ByGoAnchor, silently re-pins. Mitigation: the golden byte-equality test plus the existing pinned-hash test stay green unmodified, and the plant stays ByIndex.
- inv 3: TraitMap resolution scans ordered Vecs only via genome.locus, go_refs.contains, and a param scan, with no RNG; ByGoAnchor ties are broken by first-in-Vec-order; no HashMap.
- inv 2: the TraitMap and express stay in genome and sim-core as data plus a pure fn; ecoli.json is inert data loaded via SpeciesSpec build; no genotype-to-phenotype logic in godot.
- inv 1 and license: BiGG e_coli_core carries a UCSD non-commercial caveat contradicting CC BY 4.0. Ring-0 ecoli.json is license-clean from NCBI public-domain CDS plus CC SO/GO plus b-number facts; BiGG GPR and stoichiometry stay in the Ring-1 sidecar or oracle-fba. Record attribution and the caveat in DECISIONS.md before vendoring BiGG content.
- inv 5: keep expression behind GenotypePhenotypeMap so OntologyMap, WeightedSumMap, and subprocess maps are swappable without touching sim-core selection; the delegating wrapper preserves the seam and both concrete call sites.
- inv 6 and 7: the TraitMap is species-granular, never per-organism; pin the NCBI assembly accession, the UniProt release, and the b-number-sorted 136-gene roster in the ADR before the first ecoli.json row; LocusId order is locked by sorted b-number since a re-sort invalidates the Ring-1 sidecar keyed by LocusId.
- Hidden default: selection reads phenotype get GrowthRate unwrap_or 0.5, so a TraitMap omitting GrowthRate silently uses 0.5. Mitigation: build-time validation of exactly one GrowthRate binding.
- Pre-F2 mis-expression: feeding the 136-locus genome to the current positional map reads ecoli L0 P0 talB as plant GrowthRate, deterministic but wrong. Do not wire ecoli.json into selection until F2 lands; treat it as an inert build and round-trip fixture.
- E. coli GrowthRate WT value sets population dynamics, and an invented value undermines perfect oversight. Mitigation: derive it from the FBA biomass objective at bake time and record provenance.
